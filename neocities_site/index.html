Welcome to My Page!

Toothspikes is my own personal website where I can experiment with funky aesthetics without having to worry about professionalism. It is, or will be, a representation of myself online, showing all of my interests, hobbies, and coding skillz!! I have this project on the back burner, however, in favor of a new project I am trying to start. I will occasionally add to my blogs, and add new content to the website, but my priorities are elsewhere at the moment. Having said that, enjoy looking through the little that I do have! I've worked hard on it and it represents all of my knowledge in basic html, css, and javascript!
