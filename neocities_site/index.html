Welcome to My Page!

Toothspikes is my own personal website where I can document aspects of my coding journey and experiment with the basics of front-end coding. I created it at the end of September, which, at the point of me writing this, is about three months before I'm writing this. Before this website, the only experience I had with HTML, CSS, or JavaScript was with SpaceHey and an hour long video of the basics of HTML. Three months still isn't much of a difference, and I took over a month off to focus on coding for a C++ college class. Having said that, it's officially winter break and I'm excited to slowly add to my page again!
